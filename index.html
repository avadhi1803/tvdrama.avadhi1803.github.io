My Favourite TV Dramas

Description

According to Netflix India, in 2020, there was a 370 per cent growth in the Indian viewership of Korean television serials, fondly known as K-dramas. The key to the popularity of K-dramas lies in the storytelling. Determined to woo the female audience in Korea, its television industry has actively hired women writers. In 2019, Forbes reported that almost 90 per cent of K-drama writers were women. From zombie historical dramas to crime procedurals and romances, women are writing it all. Consequently, strong female characters, sensitive male characters who support powerful women, and a minimum of two swoon-worthy male leads per show are the norm in K-drama. The tactic is simple and effective: come for the pretty faces, stay for the storytelling.
